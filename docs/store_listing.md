**EN:**

Us is an app built for exactly two people: you and your partner. No feed, no followers — just the two of you.

**What you get:**

🗓️ **Plan dates** — a shared calendar for date nights and small moments, with reminders so they actually happen.

💡 **Weekly date ideas** — AI-generated suggestions tailored to your lifestyle, budget and interests. You can add your own ideas too.

📸 **Memories** — keep photos and moments from your relationship in one place, visible only to the two of you.

💬 **Resolve together** — an AI conversation helper that helps you talk about the hard stuff calmly and constructively. (Not therapy — but a good place to start.)

🔒 **Private, actually private** — your content is shared only between the two of you. Data is stored in the EU, and you can delete your account and all content at any time.

**Getting started:**
1. Download the app and sign in
2. Invite your partner with a code
3. Start collecting memories and planning dates

Us is for every couple — newly in love, living together, or married for 20 years. A little more us, a little less everything else.
