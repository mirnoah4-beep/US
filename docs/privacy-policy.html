Privacy Policy – US

Last updated: June 3, 2026

US is a couples app built to help partners stay close. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how you can control it.

1. What We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to run the app:

  • Name and email address — used to identify your account and send transactional emails (e.g. email verification).
  • Couple and relationship data — dates, plans, ideas, and activity logs you create inside the app.
  • Profile photo — if you choose to upload one.
  • Device information — device type, operating system version, and a push notification token, used to deliver notifications.
  • Crash reports and diagnostics — anonymised crash logs collected via Firebase Crashlytics to help us fix bugs.
  • Usage analytics — aggregated, anonymised events (e.g. "user logged in") via Firebase Analytics. We do not track individual behaviour for advertising.

We do not collect location data, contacts, or any data from other apps on your device.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide and improve the US app:

  • To authenticate you and sync your data across devices.
  • To deliver push notifications about shared plans and ideas.
  • To generate personalised weekly date ideas using AI (see Third Parties below).
  • To fix bugs and improve app stability using crash reports.

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your data with any third party except those listed in section 3.

3. Third-Party Services

US uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Functions, Messaging, Crashlytics, Analytics, App Check) — data is stored on Google servers in the EU (europe-west1 region). Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI — when generating weekly date ideas, a prompt is sent to OpenAI's API. No personally identifiable information (name, email, or relationship data) is included in these prompts. OpenAI Privacy Policy.
  • Google Sign-In / Apple Sign-In — if you choose to sign in with Google or Apple, those providers handle authentication and share only your name and email with us.

4. Data Security

All data in transit is encrypted using TLS. Data at rest is encrypted by Google Firebase using AES-256. Access to your data is restricted to your account and, where applicable, your linked partner's account. Firebase App Check is active on all app releases to prevent unauthorised API access.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — you can view your profile and relationship data directly inside the app.
  • Deletion — you can permanently delete your account and all associated data from Settings → Account → Delete Account. Deletion is immediate and irreversible.
  • Data portability — you can request a copy of your data by contacting us at support@usapp.no. We will respond within 30 days.
  • Correction — you can update your name and profile photo inside the app at any time.
  • GDPR (EEA residents) — if you are located in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation, including the right to object to processing and the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. Our lawful basis for processing your data is the performance of the contract (providing the app service).

6. Children

US is intended for users aged 16 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 16. If you believe a child under 16 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at support@usapp.no and we will delete it promptly.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all personal data — including your profile, relationship data, plans, and activity logs — is permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days. Aggregated, anonymised analytics data may be retained for longer as it cannot be linked back to you.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify you inside the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or requests regarding your privacy, please contact us at:
support@usapp.no
